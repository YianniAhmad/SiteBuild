Islamic Study

What is Islam?

Islam is a monotheistic religion revealed to the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ over 1400 years ago. It teaches that there is only one God (Allah) and that Muhammad ﷺ is His final Messenger.

The Five Pillars of Islam

  • Shahadah: Declaration of faith
  • Salah: Prayer 5 times daily
  • Zakah: Giving to charity
  • Sawm: Fasting in Ramadan
  • Hajj: Pilgrimage to Makkah
